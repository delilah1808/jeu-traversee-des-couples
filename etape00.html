The game's rules

Welcome to the couples crossing, a game of logic and strategy that will require you to think carefully before acting! Come solve the couples dilemma if you’re ready Three couples must cross a river. A boat found there can only carry two people. If a woman can’t stay with other men without her husband’s presence, how are they going to arrange the back and forth so husbands don’t get jealous ? It’s up to you to solve this problem !
